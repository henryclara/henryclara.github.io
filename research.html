Overview

I am investigating the dynamics of ice rises and ice rumples in coastal Antarctica. These features act to regulate the flow of ice into the Southern Ocean. Under a changing climate, the geometry and ice flow dynamics of ice rises and ice rumples change, causing a change in their buttressing effect.

Tools

In order to investigate ice rises and ice rumples in an idealised sense, we use the finite element model Elmer/Ice.
